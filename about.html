About Fresh Connect

Fresh Connect is a web-based platform dedicated to supporting India's street food vendors by simplifying how they find and connect with trusted, affordable raw material suppliers.

Vendors often rely on local connections, uncertain pricing, and word-of-mouth deals. This leads to:

  • Fluctuating supply quality and costs
  • Lack of supplier verification or trust
  • No digital tools for price comparison or reviews

Fresh_Connect solves this by:

  • Providing a curated list of verified suppliers
  • Offering real-time access to vendor info & pricing
  • Allowing vendors to leave reviews and build trust
